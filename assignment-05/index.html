Skillet Surprise!

Clear Out the Undesirables
in Your Fridge in One Fell Swoop!

Ingredients:

(Subject to Change depending on Fridge)
  • Eggs
  • Beets
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Mushrooms
  • Any green stuff nearing the limits of palatability

Directions:

  1. Sautee onions and garlics--mmm, aromatics!
  2. Crack a couple eggs in and add S*P to taste, stirring as you feel.
  3. Toss in the mushrooms, greens, and beets just to warm them up for the scramble.
  4. Dump on plate and garnish with the freshest greens to beautify for your Insty.
